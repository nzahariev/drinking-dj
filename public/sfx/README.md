# Sound effects (SFX)

Place your sound effect files here. The app looks for files named by sound ID with `.mp3` or `.wav` extension.

**Required filenames (one of .mp3 or .wav per ID):**

- `airhorn` — Air Horn
- `bruh` — Bruh
- `boo` — Boo
- `cheer` — Cheer
- `drumfill` — Drum Fill
- `rimshot` — Rimshot
- `scratch` — Record Scratch
- `drink` — "Drink!" bell
- `applause` — Applause
- `whistle` — Whistle
- `laser` — Laser
- `ok` — "OK!" voice

Example: `airhorn.mp3`, `cheer.wav`, etc.

Use royalty-free or your own recordings. Do not use material you don't have rights to.
